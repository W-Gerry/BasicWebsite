Concepts Learned

Accessibility & Responsive Design

Accessibility

I believe a key aspect of web development is creating applications that as many people as possible are able to access and use as intended. Ensuring that accessibility concepts are considered during the development process is key to a successful application.

  • Perceivable
  • Operable
  • Understandable
  • Robust

Responsive design

In addition to ensuring that web applications are accessible, it is important to recognise that there are a wide variety of devices and operating systems that need to be able to interact with your application so applying Responsive Design principles throughout the entire development process is also crucial.
